The founder of STEM Seeds is an alum of Summer Academy of Math and Science at Carnegie Mellon University, and connected with Building Bridges Initiative, a Pittsburgh organization made to connect different communities, to create a Building Bridges Event! With high school students from over 20 different states, incredibly designed bridges were formed with basic understanding of engineering and with the encouragement that comes with community.
